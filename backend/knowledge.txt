Innovatech Solutions is a leading AI startup headquartered in Bengaluru, India. Founded in 2021 by data scientist Priya Sharma, the company's mission is to democratize machine learning. Their flagship product, released in 2024, is called 'Synapse'. Synapse is a no-code data analytics platform that allows businesses to build and deploy predictive models without writing any code. The platform has seen rapid adoption in the finance and retail sectors.
